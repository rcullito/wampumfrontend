How does Wampum choose a destination for my clothes?

We think it is pretty cool to get as targeted a match for your item as possible. So for instance, if you are donating jeans, and there is a location on our list that specailizes PURELY in accepting jeans, we'll suggest that location first over say, a donation center that accepts all types of clothing.

Having said that, we also think that choice is a good concept, so you can click "shuffle" to mix up the suggested donation location for your item. If you are interested in the underlying concept behind our suggestion decisions please see: tf-idf.
