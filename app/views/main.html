About

Wampum is a Native American word for shell beads that were used as a form of currency. Wampum.io is a project aimed at providing an incentive for disposing of your old clothes responsibly. For a full list of the locations we support, see here.

Wampum.io is made in Cambridge, Massachusetts. For any questions at all please email rob@wampum.io. Or to find out more about what we're up to, check out the blog.
