Wampum Is

A search engine for anyone looking to get rid of old clothes or household items. The search results are comprised of NGOs and socially conscious ventures that will put your old things to good use.

Why Wampum?

All too often, it's easier to throw something in the trash than it is to think about how to get rid of it responsibly. Wampum uncovers a new home for an item in milliseconds by making it simple and easy to search for locations that will accept it.

How does it work?

Wampum assigns a higher weight to donation centers that specialize in just the item that was searched for, although facilities that accept a wide variety of items (such as the Salvation Army) are listed too. This ranking philosophy is based on the idea that a specialized, personal connection to the end recipient of the product is a powerful motivator in getting individuals to part with their unused belongings.
