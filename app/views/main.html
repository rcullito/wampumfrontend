  • About

Wampum.io is a project aimed at providing an incentive for disposing of your old clothes responsibly. We pass along all of the clothes you send to locations that either use the goods for donations or as the basis for some neat upcycling projects(think old jeans become denim insulation in someone's home).

/wämpəm/ noun: Native American term for shells that were used as a form of currency.

  • How it works

When you place an order with Wampum, we send you a box and shipping label so that you can mail your old clothes from your home to Wampum's warehouse. Once we receive your clothes, Wampum will mail you a gift card from a retailer you select as a thank you. We pass all of the inventory along to these forward thinking and environmentally minded locations.
