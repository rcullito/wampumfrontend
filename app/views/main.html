Oyster Pail

Recycling To Go.

We're working on scaling up a pail from the take out food size above to about the size of a laundry hamper. Drop in clothes, electronics, and other items you don't need any more. When the pail is full, you ship it to us and we redistribute the goods to their second homes. Track where your "take out" is headed through our online dashboard.
