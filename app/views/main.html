Trade in your old clothes for gift cards at the stores you bought them from.

About

Wampum.io is a project aimed at providing an incentive for disposing of your old clothes responsibly. We pass along all of the clothes you send to locations that either use the goods for donations or as the basis for some neat upcycling projects(think old jeans become denim insulation in someone's home).

Wampum.io is made in Cambridge, Massachusetts. For any questions at all please email rob@wampum.io. Or to find out more about what we're up to, check out the blog.

/wämpəm/ noun: Native American term for shell beads that were used as a form of currency.
